How does the free trial work?
Most plans offer a free trial period (typically 7-14 days) where you can explore all features without any commitment. No credit card is required to start. You can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel at any time during or after the trial.
Can I change my plan later?
Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. When upgrading, you'll get immediate access to new features. When downgrading, changes take effect at the end of your current billing period.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), bKash, Nagad, Rocket, and bank transfers. All payments are processed securely through our payment partners.
Is my data secure?
Absolutely! We use bank-level encryption, regular security audits, and comply with data protection regulations. Your data is stored in secure, SOC 2 certified data centers with automatic backups.
Do you offer customer support?
Yes! All plans include 24/7 email support. Higher-tier plans include priority email support, live chat, and dedicated account managers. Our support team is knowledgeable about social commerce and ready to help you succeed.
