How overrides work

  • An override applies on top of the user's role.
  • A Grant gives a permission the user does not have.
  • A Deny blocks a permission even if a role grants it.
  • If both Grant and Deny exist for the same user+permission, Deny wins.
  • Cross-store overrides are reserved for super admins.
